The Privacy policy

The iot-proxy.com is intended to help users to create scenarios to manage eWeLink-based devices via Blockly-like schemes. The usual workflow is: 1. You provide us access to your eWeLink account. The password/login/region are stored using the Local Storage of the browser in encrypted form. The data used to get the list of your eWeLink devices. Your password and login are not stored on the server-side, we use them only to send requests to eWeLink servers. Your eWeLink password and email are also stored as part of generated App script, so that script will be able to manage your devices. 2. As soon as we got the list of devices, you may create the scenario using Blockly. The typical scenario consists of several checks and several commands to change the state of eWeLink devices. The scenario is intended to be executed periodically using the Google App scripts. 3. Then you need to login into your Google account using the “Sign in” button. The application is intended to create Google scripts based on the Blockly scheme, so we need access to create App scripts and Spreadsheets or delete them by your request. Also, we need access to the Google Drive app folder to store your projects. 4. We don’t collect your personal information related to Google account on our servers. We store the current project in the browser Local Storage and on Google Drive App data. The project consists of blocks configuration, spreadsheet reference, script reference. The project does not contain your eWeLink login or password, does not contain your email or profile data. 5. As soon as you logged into your Google account and given all permissions, the application tries to find your projects stored in the Google Drive App folder and opens them. 6. When you do any changes to your project they will be saved automatically to the Google Drive App folder. 7. As soon as you need to create a Google App script based on your blocks, you need to press “Google Sheets integration”. Then the application will create the spreadsheet and script based on your blocks. That is why we need permissions to create and update Google spreadsheets and scripts. 8. We don’t share any information you shared with us. We never send any information provided by users to other users. 9. We collect basic information (your e-mail) on our server just for usage statistics.
